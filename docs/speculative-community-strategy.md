# KaufCare — Community Pillar Strategy (Speculative Research)

**Clinic:** KaufCare Advanced Urgent Care
**Location:** 2515 Eliot St, Denver, CO 80211 (Jefferson Park)
**Operator:** Dr. Kaufman (board-certified ER physician)
**Model:** Cash-pay only, 80+ services, walk-in 7 days/week
**Hours:** Mon–Fri 9–5, Sat–Sun 10–2

---

## What Makes KaufCare Differentiated

Most urgent cares treat scrapes and strep. KaufCare is something else: an ER-trained physician running a cash-pay clinic that also offers IV therapy, ketamine, regenerative medicine, and 80+ services — at 70–90% below ER cost. That's not urgent care. That's a neighborhood clinic that punches at hospital weight without the hospital price or the wait.

The differentiation story writes itself. The job is getting the neighborhood to hear it.

---

## Speculative Methodologies for Community Pillaring

### 1. Own the "Anti-ER" Narrative
Jefferson Park residents have ER horror stories — $800 copays, 4-hour waits for a sprained ankle. KaufCare is the answer to that problem, and that message should be everywhere: door hangers, local NextDoor posts, neighborhood Facebook groups, Google ads. "We're not the ER. We're better."

Speculative tactic: price comparison content. Post a "what this would cost at the ER vs. here" breakdown for common visits (laceration, UTI, IV fluids). This gets shared.

### 2. Jefferson Park Neighborhood Domination
Before going broad, go deep in the immediate neighborhood. Jefferson Park is walkable, tight-knit, young, active.

- Table at the Jefferson Park Farmer's Market
- Sponsor the Jefferson Park neighborhood newsletter / NextDoor presence
- Door hanger campaign within 1-mile radius: "Your neighborhood urgent care"
- Signage partnerships with adjacent businesses
- Show up at neighborhood association meetings

Speculative goal: every Jefferson Park resident knows KaufCare exists before they need it.

### 3. Employer & Occupational Health Pipeline
Denver has a large trades and construction workforce — the exact demographic that needs occupational medicine, drug screening, and injury care.

- Build a simple "workplace account" program: employers get a direct billing contact and priority visits
- Target construction companies, restaurants, warehouses, and small business clusters within 3 miles
- Offer a free first occupational visit for new employer partners
- AI angle: use AI to identify, research, and draft outreach to 50+ Denver employers in a day

### 4. Sports Physicals & Youth Athletics
Denver is obsessed with youth sports. Sports physicals are a high-volume, low-complexity service that gets families in the door and builds loyalty.

- Price a sports physical package competitively ($30–40)
- Reach out to Jefferson Park, Sunnyside, and LoHi youth leagues
- Sponsor one or two local youth teams
- This converts to: "that's our team's doctor"

### 5. Advanced Services as a Premium Hook
Most people in the neighborhood don't know there's a ketamine clinic and IV therapy bar two blocks away.

- Separate content/marketing for IV therapy and ketamine tracks
- Partner with local fitness studios and yoga studios for recovery IV referrals
- Ketamine therapy: partner with therapists and mental health practices for referrals

### 6. Price Transparency as Brand Identity
KaufCare's cash-pay model is a competitive weapon.

- Full "menu" of service prices on the website and in the clinic
- "No surprise billing. Ever." as a brand promise
- Post pricing to social media — people screenshot and share this content

### 7. The 24-Hour Follow-Up System
A simple, AI-driven follow-up text 24 hours after a visit creates a loyalty flywheel that no competitor is running. Also the top driver of 5-star reviews.
