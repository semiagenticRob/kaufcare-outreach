Strategic implications

It's the neighborhood, not an employer

1,633 businesses / 15,860 employees in-zip, but residents work elsewhere (avg 26-min commute). LoHi's walkability, dining scene, and lifestyle brand are the draw — not a single company.

The audience refreshes annually

~10,100 move in, ~9,550 move out each year. Marketing is never "done." Apartment welcome packets, leasing office relationships, and recurring activations compound; one-shot campaigns don't.

16 apartment buildings = your pipeline

~2,000 units within ½ mile. 5 managed by Cornerstone (1 relationship = 5 buildings). Skyline (533 units), 2785 Speer (332), Infinity (273), Alcott (248) are highest-density targets.
